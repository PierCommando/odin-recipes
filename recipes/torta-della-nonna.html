Torta della Nonna

Description

This is a description of a torta della nonna, wich I really like and in my family we're used to eat a lot of it. Not so much anymore since I got out of my parents' house.

Ingredients

  • Shortcrust
  • Cream
  • Pine Nuts
  • Powdered Sugar

Steps

  1. Line a baking pan with a 3/5 mm layer of Shortcrust
  2. Place the Cream inside
  3. Cover with another layer of Shortcrust
  4. Wet the layer and place a bunch of Pine Nuts on it
  5. In the oven 1 hour
  6. Take out and cover with Powdered Sugar
