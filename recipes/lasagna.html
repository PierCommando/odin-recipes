Lasagna

Description

This is a description of a lasagna, wich I really like and in my family we're used to eat a lot of it. Not so much anymore since I got out of my parents' house.

Ingredients

  • Sfoglia
  • Besciamella
  • Ragu
  • Parmigiano

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Wet the bottom of a casserole with a couple spoons of ragu
  3. Put layer of pasta
  4. Put layer of ragu
  5. Put layer of besciamella
  6. Start again from pasta
  7. Reach the top
  8. Grate a lot of Parmigiano on top
  9. In the oven 1 hour
  10. Take out and eat!
