Bistecca alla Fiorentina

Description

This is a description of a Bistecca alla Fiorentina, wich I really like and in my family we're used to eat a lot of it. Not so much anymore since I got out of my parents' house.

Ingredients

  • Bistecca
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the fire/li>
  2. Put the Bistecca on the grill once the fire is super hot but with no flames
  3. 5 minutes per side max
  4. Optional - Place it straight on its bone for 3/5 minutes
  5. Take out and let it rest for 5 more minutes
  6. Cut in big chunks and eat it!
